Privacy Policy

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy

Flying Star Immigration Services Inc. ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide to us, including:

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Identification documents (passport, birth certificate, ID)
  • Immigration-related documents (education credentials, work history, language tests, police certificates, medical records)
  • Financial information for payment processing
  • Any other information you provide when using our services

We also collect information automatically when you visit our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited through cookies and similar technologies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide immigration consulting services
  • Prepare and submit applications to immigration authorities
  • Communicate with you about your case
  • Process payments
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Improve our services
  • Send you updates and newsletters (with your consent)

3. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

  • Canadian immigration authorities (IRCC, provincial programs)
  • Third-party service providers (language testing centers, medical facilities, translation services, payment processors)
  • Legal and regulatory bodies when required by law

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations, typically 7 years after case closure.

6. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings.
