Required Documents Checklist

  • Valid Passport: Must be valid for the duration of your stay.
  • Letter of Acceptance: From a Designated Learning Institution (DLI).
  • Proof of Funds: Tuition fees + living expenses ($10,000/year).
  • Immigration Medical Exam (IME): From a panel physician.
  • Letter of Explanation (SOP): Stating your study goals.
  • English Proficiency: IELTS/PTE/TOEFL scores (typically 6.0+ bands).
