Privacy Policies

We collect information that you provide to us, including contact details (name, email, phone number, address), identification documents (passport, birth certificate, ID), and immigration-related documents (education credentials, work history, language tests). We also collect data automatically through cookies to improve our services.

We use your information to provide immigration consulting services, prepare and submit applications to IRCC, communicate with you about your case, process payments, and comply with legal obligations.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. We retain your data for as long as necessary, typically 7 years after case closure, in accordance with legal requirements for immigration consultants.

We may share your information with Canadian immigration authorities (IRCC) and specific third-party service providers (like language testing centers or payment processors) strictly when necessary for your application. We never sell your personal information.
