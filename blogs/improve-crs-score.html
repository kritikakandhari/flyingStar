How to Improve Your CRS Score in 2026

  1. Home
  2. Blogs
  3. CRS Boost

The Express Entry system remains the primary pathway for economic immigration to Canada. In 2026, staying competitive means optimizing every point available in your CRS (Comprehensive Ranking System) profile.

1. Retake Your Language Tests

Even a small increase in your CLB level (from 8 to 9) can lead to a massive jump in your overall CRS score due to the way skill transferability points are calculated. Language proficiency is the most flexible factor you can improve.

2. Get a Second Degree or Certificate

If you only have a Bachelor's degree, adding a one-year post-graduate certificate can significantly boost your education points. Dual-credentialed candidates are highly favored in the Express Entry pool.

3. Provincial Nomination (PNP)

A provincial nomination adds a guaranteed 600 points to your profile, practically ensuring an Invitation to Apply (ITA) in the next draw. Many provinces have specific streams for tech professionals, healthcare workers, and French speakers.
