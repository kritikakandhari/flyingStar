Canada New Immigration Levels Plan for 2026

  1. Home
  2. Blogs
  3. 2026 Plan

Canada has recently unveiled its ambitious Immigration Levels Plan for 2026, signaling a continued commitment to welcoming newcomers to support economic growth and strengthen communities across the country.

The new plan focuses on prioritizing candidates with skills in high-demand sectors such as healthcare, trades, and technology. This targeted approach aims to address labor shortages while ensuring that new immigrants have the best chance of successful integration into the Canadian workforce.

Key Targets for 2026

The Government of Canada has set a target of welcoming over 500,000 new permanent residents in 2026. A significant portion of these admissions will be directed through the Express Entry system and Provincial Nominee Programs (PNPs), ensuring that candidates with the right skill sets are prioritized.

  • Economic Class: Approximately 60% of all admissions.
  • Family Class: Supporting reunification of families.
  • Refugees and Humanitarian Class: Maintaining Canada's traditions.

What This Means for You

If you are planning to migrate to Canada, 2026 presents a significant window of opportunity. The increased targets mean that more spaces are available for qualified candidates. However, competition remains high, and having a professionally handled application is more important than ever to stand out in the pool.

At Flying Star Immigration, we stay updated with every policy change to ensure your application aligns perfectly with the current IRCC requirements. Whether you are applying through Express Entry or seeking a Provincial nomination, our experts are here to guide you every step of the way.
